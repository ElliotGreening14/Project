Dallas Cowboys defeat the New Orleans Saints

In an astonishing Monday night football outcome, the lowly Dallas Cowboys who were beginning to fret over the future direction, pulled out an unlikely 13-10 victory over the predicted Super Bowl-bound New Orleans Saints. A defensive masterclass was showcased to NFL Fans, as the MVP-leading candidate Drew Brees had a low-par performance and threw an unlikely and untimely interception to seal the win for the Cowboys.
